关于我 About Me

我是一名计算机科学领域的学生，目前就读于会津大学计算机信息系统专业。我的研究方向主要是联邦学习、机器学习和数据分析，尤其关注隐私保护和分布式智能系统的设计与实现。 I am a Ph.D. student in the field of Computer Science, currently studying Computer Information Systems at the University of Aizu in Japan. My research focuses on federated learning, machine learning, and data analysis, with particular attention to privacy protection and the design and implementation of distributed intelligent systems.

在攻读博士学位之前，我获得了会津大学计算机信息系统硕士学位和郑州大学计算机信息管理学士学位。拥有实际工程项目经验，曾参与多个机器学习和数据分析相关的研究项目，并在国际学术会议和期刊上发表论文。 Before pursuing my Ph.D., I earned a Master's degree in Computer Information Systems from the University of Aizu and a Bachelor's degree in Computer Information Management from Zhengzhou University. I have practical engineering project experience, having participated in several research projects related to machine learning and data analysis, and have published papers in international academic conferences and journals.

我的目标是通过研究和创新，推动人工智能技术在保护用户隐私的前提下，实现更广泛的应用和发展。我相信技术的进步应该与社会的需求和伦理标准相结合，创造出真正有益于人类的智能系统。 My goal is to promote the wider application and development of artificial intelligence technology while protecting user privacy through research and innovation. I believe that technological progress should be combined with social needs and ethical standards to create intelligent systems that truly benefit humanity.
