学术背景Academic Background

徐建波，男，博士，现任海南大学计算机科学与技术学院副研究员。于2022年3月获得日本会津大学计算机与信息系统学博士学位，研究方向主要包括机器学习、信号处理和生理信号的无缝监测等领域。Jianbo Xu, male, PhD, currently an Associate Researcher at the School of Computer Science and Technology, Hainan University. He received his Ph.D. degree in Computer and Information Systems from the University of Aizu, Japan in March 2022. His research interests include Machine Learning, Signal Processing, and Seamless Physiological Signal Monitoring.

在国际会议和期刊上发表多篇论文，研究工作主要聚焦于利用深度学习方法进行ECG信号处理和个人身份识别，特别关注不同环境下（如洗浴时不同水温）的ECG信号特征及其在身份验证中的应用。He has published multiple papers in international conferences and journals. His research work primarily focuses on using deep learning methods for ECG signal processing and personal identification, particularly focusing on the ECG signal characteristics in different environments (such as different water temperatures during bathing) and their application in identity verification.

目前在海南大学开展教学与研究工作，致力于将理论研究成果应用于实际生活场景，推动生理信号监测技术的发展与应用。Currently, he is engaged in teaching and research at Hainan University, dedicated to applying theoretical research results to real-life scenarios, and promoting the development and application of physiological signal monitoring technology.

研究方向Research Interests

机器学习Machine Learning

应用深度学习算法处理和分析复杂数据，特别是在卷积神经网络(CNN)在生理信号处理中的应用研究。Applying deep learning algorithms to process and analyze complex data, especially research on the application of Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) in physiological signal processing.

信号处理Signal Processing

研究生理信号（如心电图ECG）的特征提取与分析技术，开发高效的信号处理算法。Researching feature extraction and analysis techniques for physiological signals (such as ECG), and developing efficient signal processing algorithms.

生理信号的无缝监测Seamless Physiological Signal Monitoring

探索在各种生活场景（如沐浴）下实现生理信号的无缝监测技术，研究环境因素对生理信号监测的影响。Exploring seamless physiological signal monitoring technology in various life scenarios (such as bathing), and studying the impact of environmental factors on physiological signal monitoring.

基于生理信号的身份识别Physiological Signal-based Identity Recognition

探索利用ECG等生理信号进行个人身份识别的方法，提高不同环境条件下的识别准确率。Exploring methods for personal identification using physiological signals such as ECG, and improving recognition accuracy under different environmental conditions.
